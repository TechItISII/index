// 01 — ABOUT US

Engineering
and Passion

We are Team Tech it ISII, a team of students passionate about robotics, engineering and technology. We participate every year in the FIRST Tech Challenge, an international competition that tests our design, programming and teamwork skills.

Our goal is not only to win — but to grow as individuals, develop real STEM skills and inspire other young people to discover the world of technology.

🤖 Robotics 💻 Development ⚙️ Assembly 🎨 Design
⚡

Our Mission

Inspire young people to become the scientific and technological leaders of tomorrow, through the FIRST program.

🏆

FTC Season

Every year we face a new challenge with a completely different field and rules. Adapting is our superpower.

FIRST Ideals — Gracious Professionalism®

Meet APEX

Technical Specifications

Chassis Aluminum 6061-T6 · 18×18"
Motors 4× REV HD Hex · 312 RPM
Drive Train Mecanum Wheels 4WD
Controller REV Control Hub v1.1
Programming Language Java · FTC SDK
Autonomous AprilTags + OpenCV
Weight 15.4 kg

📷 Vision processing
📡 Odometry 3 wheels
